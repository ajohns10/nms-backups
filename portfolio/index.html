Accelerate WordPress Website

PHP, HTML, & CSS

Accelerate's vision for this project was to revamp their current website, make it polished, and improve functionality. They wanted to be able to showcase case studies of their featured work and an excerpt of their most recent blog post on the homepage. In order to revamp and display a blog excerpt the current blog had to be carefully imported from another blogging platform to ensure no content was lost. Now, their current and potential clients have easy access to information about how to save time within their business, increase sales, increase the happiness of their own customers, and also about Accelerate's company.
